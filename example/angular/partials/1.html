lfswsrelay.js An example, using Angular.js

Connect to the Relay

lfswsrelay.js is a convenience wrapper to talk to the LFSWorld InSim Relay, using WebSockets.

It takes care of connecting you, and decoding the packets, allowing you to get on with writing your application.

Step one is to create an instance of the lfswsrelay.js client and connect to the relay.
